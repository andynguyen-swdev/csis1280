Shanghai, China - July 2019

Contrasting with the cold atmosphere of Beijing, Shanghai has a dynamic vibe similar to that of Hong Kong. After all, it's not a political center but a trade center. It's a lovely place if you are rich and have a lot of money to spend, unlike me (insert sad emoji here). Ultimately all I could do is window shopping. I also visited a temple with a lot of cats chlling inside. If only our lives were so comfortable.

Beijing, China - July 2019

Beijing - or Peking - the capital of People's Repulbic of China - the most populous capital in the world (source) - the city where the Forbidden City is located at - the place where people seem a bit cold although it's very hot in the summer. Climbing the Great Wall was a remarkable experience. It took one hell of an effort for me to get through 3 posts, which is not even one thousandth of the total.

Bangkok, Thailand - April 2019

The most visited city in the world. Not Paris. Not London. But Bangkok ???

The main reason that I went to Bangkok for was BTS's concert. If you don't know about this band then you must have been living under the rock for the last few years (or you haven't been in East Asia). There were so many people going to this concert that I had trouble getting to the stadium where it was hosted. The traffic jam was so bad that I had to walk on my feet for an hour each way. The concert was dope nonetheless.
